MoMI From Home

The recent closure of our building in an effort to mitigate the spread of COVID-19 has made a lasting impact on the Museum and the communities we serve, both locally and internationally. As an institution devoted to the moving image — an important conduit for both information and escape in the current moment — we are exploring new ways to continue serving our communities and fulfifilling our mission.

We find ourselves in a new world, navigating newfound opportunities while preparing for setbacks as best we can. Now more than ever we need your support.
